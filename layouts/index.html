Project OHCOE (Oral Health Care in the Opioid Epidemic)

Project OHCOE trains dentists to help patients combat opioid addiction. Get Started
Project OHCOE is an online initiative created by Columbia University to help dental trainees and practitioners confidently identify patients at risk for opioid addiction, interact with them, and manage their treatment.
